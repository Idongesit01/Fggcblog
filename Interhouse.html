3rd Annual General Meeting

On the 15th of April, 2023, the annual interhouse sports competition was conducted by Federal Government Girls College (FGGC) IOI. The event was attended by students, teachers, and The Federal Government Girls College (FGGC) IOI Alumnae Association . The sports competition featured a variety of events such as track and field events, relay races, long jump, high jump, and shot put.
The competition was held between four houses: Red House, Blue House, Green House, and Yellow House. The sports competition was a fun-filled day of friendly competition and camaraderie among the students. The competition was very competitive, with each house trying to outdo the other. The Blue House emerged as the overall winner of the competition, scoring the most points across all the events.
The presence of The Federal Government Girls College (FGGC) IOI Alumnae Association was a great encouragement to the current students, who were able to interact with the former students and learn from their experiences. It also provided an opportunity for the students to network and learn from successful individuals who had passed through the school. The inter-house sports competition was a huge success, and the students displayed great enthusiasm and sportsmanship throughout the day.The Federal Government Girls College (FGGC) IOI Alumnae Association was not left out on all the fun.
The competition provided a platform for the students to showcase their athletic abilities, fostered healthy competition among the houses, and showcased the unity and pride of the students in their school.

Valedictory Service & Prize-giving Day

The Valedictory Service & Prize-giving Day of the academic year 2022 was a remarkable and momentous occasion not only for the graduating students (Class of 2022) but also for the Alumnae Association, which sponsored the Excellence Awards for the first time. The event highlighted the academic achievements of the graduating students and The debut of the Alumnae-sponsored Excellence Awards which will serve as a motivation for the students to strive for excellence in their academics, and the teachers will continue to provide quality education and nurture the students' talents.

The Debut of the Alumnae-sponsored Excellence Awards

One of the most significant events of the day was the debut of the Alumnae-sponsored Excellence Awards. The Association, along with individual members, sponsored awards for various subjects, and the winners were awarded cash prizes. This initiative was a novel idea, and everyone was thrilled with it. The Excellence Awards served as a motivation for the students to strive for excellence in their academics. The gesture was well appreciated, and the Principal, teachers, and students expressed their joy and gratitude.

Students' Exhilaration

The Valedictory Service & Prize-giving Day was an emotional event, especially for the graduating students who were bidding farewell to their alma mater. The students were thrilled to receive their certificates and prizes, and they expressed their joy and gratitude. The day was a testament to the hard work and dedication of the students, teachers, and the school administration. The Principal and teachers were proud of the students' achievements and encouraged them to continue to excel in their future endeavors.

Maintaining the Excellence Awards as a Yearly Tradition

The Alumnae Association Executive Committee plans to maintain the Excellence Awards as a yearly tradition at each Valedictory Service/ prize-giving day. This decision was well received and appreciated by everyone. This initiative will undoubtedly motivate students to strive for excellence in their academics. The yearly tradition will not only encourage the students but will also serve as a motivation for the teachers to provide quality education and nurture the students' talents.
